About Kenya Through the Lens

Every photograph tells a story. Discover the inspiration behind this photography website and the beauty that Kenya has to offer.

Our Story

Kenya Through the Lens was created to celebrate the breathtaking beauty, diverse wildlife, rich culture, and spectacular landscapes that make Kenya one of Africa's most remarkable destinations. Every image featured on this website captures unforgettable moments that inspire exploration, appreciation, and conservation of our natural heritage.

Our Mission

Inspire

Encourage people around the world to discover Kenya through captivating photography.

Explore

Highlight Kenya's national parks, mountains, beaches, cities, and hidden gems.

Conserve

Promote appreciation and protection of Kenya's incredible wildlife and environment.

Fun Facts About Kenya

  • Kenya is home to over 50 national parks and reserves.
  • The Great Migration is one of the Seven Natural Wonders of Africa.
  • Mount Kenya is Africa's second-highest mountain.
  • Kenya has more than 40 different ethnic communities.
  • Swahili and English are the official languages.
